Various Types of Disasters

  • Earthquakes: Sudden ground shaking caused by tectonic activity.
  • Floods: Overflow of water onto normally dry land.
  • Hurricanes: Powerful tropical storms with strong winds and heavy rainfall.
  • Wildfires: Uncontrolled fires that spread rapidly through vegetation.
  • Tornadoes: Violent rotating columns of air that can cause severe damage.
  • Tsunamis: Large ocean waves triggered by undersea disturbances.
  • Drought: Prolonged period of abnormally dry weather leading to water scarcity.
  • Winter Storms: Severe weather events with snow, ice, and freezing temperatures.
  • Landslides: Downward movement of rocks, soil, or debris on slopes.
  • Industrial Accidents: Disasters resulting from accidents in industrial facilities.
